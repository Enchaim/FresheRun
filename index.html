The Brainchild: Freshe Run



Fresh 清新

The patented technology enables you to breathe the clean air at ease. No matter how bad the air pollution is, Freshe Run mekes it an enjoyment.

特別為戶外運動打造，Freshe Run要讓空氣污染不再掃興。專利的流體設計，讓你可以輕鬆享受乾淨空氣。

Comfortable 舒適

Unlike masks or other existing products, we provide fresh air in a comfortable way. Say goodbye to stifling hot and sweaty humid.

不像市面上其他的口罩，運動時會產生恐怖的悶熱窒息感，我們將用全新的思維提供前所未見的清新體驗。

Fashion 時尚

We devoted our passion and efforts to make exercise a truely enjoyment. Freshe Run is not only an exercise equipment, but a fashion masterpiece.

讓所有人健康自信的運動，是我們追求的目標。Freshe Run不是運動裝備，而是你最耀眼的時尚單品S。




Many urban dwellers love to jog in the park, along the riverside or around the city, for it enlightens people with the energy and passion toward life. It is one of the most important parts to many people, but the pros of running couldn’t conceal the cons that terrible air condition brought us. Excessive PM2.5 or bad smell from traffic jams would damage physically and psychologically, and increase the risk of respiratory diseases substantially.

Freshe Run redesigned the headband with hydromechanics. Image a invisible shield prevent people from polluted air, which enables jogging lovers to breathe fresh air without a pinch of discomfort. Cars on the street and polluted air would no longer be a nightmare isolating people form the beauty of the city. Besides Freshe Run headband, our team is struggling to develop strong product lines to bring runners the technology that really matters!




還記得運動時，沈浸在屬於自己的小世界裡，那簡單、平凡而幸福的感動嗎？生活在都市的我們往往選擇在住處附近的公園慢跑，但 PM2.5、空氣懸浮微粒、汽機車怠速廢氣並不會因為我們要運動就自動消失，身體跟肺都會記得。而現在一群熱愛運動的運動痴 、運動狂，要一起用科技提供前所未見的清新體驗，為大家找回運動帶來的滿滿感動。

Freshe Run 名字取自英文 Fresh She Run。我們重新設計了女生運動常用的髮帶，用流體力學設計，讓氣流在使用者臉上打造一層隱形防護罩，提供不著痕跡的清新美麗。不必犧牲運動舒適度或整體造型，就能讓路邊等紅燈的汽機車或紫爆的PM2.5不再是走出門發現這城市美好的阻礙。除了Freshe Run頭帶之外，團隊未來還會繼續打造更多專為跑者設計的智慧產品，讓科技不只是名詞，而是為你帶來健康快樂的魔法。

「#跑了就懂」是你的事，但幫你「#跑的清新」，就是我們的事了！
